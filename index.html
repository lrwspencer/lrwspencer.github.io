About Me

I am a 6th year PhD candidate in Economics at UCLA Anderson School of Management. My research focuses on Applied Microeconomics, Cultural Economics, and Economic History. I am particularly interested in the economic impacts of long-run changes in culture.

Research

My work aims to address questions about the economic impacts of cultural change using archival and primary historical data.
